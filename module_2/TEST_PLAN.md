D2) 10-Minute Test Script

English Version

Step 1 — Input (30s): "Hi, thank you so much for taking the time. I'll walk you through what we're testing today, it'll be quick. So this is an example of raw trend data pulled from Xiaohongshu. At this stage it's still pretty unfiltered, so it includes a mix of relevant signals and some noise."

Step 2 — Module (30–60s): "What our system does is take this raw data and filter it based on brand relevance, category fit, and how actionable the trend is. So instead of going through everything manually, it narrows it down to a small set of trends that are more worth paying attention to."

Step 3 — Output (2 min): "Here are the shortlisted trends. For each one, you'll see a score, a ranking, and a short explanation of why it was selected. We also include supporting evidence, like engagement signals or repeated mentions across posts, so the result is grounded rather than just a black-box output. I'll quickly walk you through one example."

Step 4 — Questions (5 min): "I'd love to get your quick thoughts on this. First, on a scale of 1 to 5, how useful are these trends for your work? Second, how much do you trust the reasoning and evidence behind them, also 1 to 5? And overall, what feels missing, off, or potentially risky to you?" Follow-ups if time allows: "Which of these trends would you actually use first, and why?" / "Does anything here feel off-brand to you?" / "Is there anything you usually look for that's not shown here?"

Step 5 — Close (2 min): "Thank you, this is really helpful. We're trying to understand how to make these outputs more practical and usable in real scenarios, so any honest feedback is super valuable."

Chinese Version

步骤 1 — 输入 (30秒): "你好，谢谢你今天抽时间帮我们测试一下，我会很快带你过一遍。这里是从小红书抓取的一些原始趋势数据，目前还没有经过筛选，所以里面会有一些相关的信息，也会有一些比较杂的内容。"

步骤 2 — 模块说明 (30–60秒): "我们这个系统的作用，就是在这些原始数据的基础上，帮你筛选出更符合品牌、品类，并且更有实际价值的趋势。也就是不用你自己去看所有信息，而是帮你缩小到几个更值得关注的趋势。"

步骤 3 — 输出展示 (2分钟): "这是我们筛选之后的趋势结果。每一个趋势都会有一个评分、一个排名，还有一个简单的解释说明为什么被选中。同时我们也会提供一些支撑证据，比如互动数据，或者多个帖子中重复出现的信号，让这个结果更有依据，而不是一个'黑箱'。我可以简单带你看一个例子。"

步骤 4 — 提问 (5分钟): "想请你帮我们简单反馈一下：第一，从1到5分，你觉得这些趋势对你的工作有多有用？第二，从1到5分，你对这些分析逻辑和证据有多信任？最后，你觉得有哪些地方是缺失的、不太对的，或者有风险的？" 追加问题：你会优先用哪一个趋势？为什么？/ 有没有哪个趋势你会觉得有点不太符合品牌？/ 有没有你平时会参考，但这里没有体现的信息？

步骤 5 — 结束 (2分钟): "谢谢你的反馈，这对我们来说真的很有帮助。我们主要是想让这个结果更贴近实际使用场景，所以任何真实的想法都非常重要。"
