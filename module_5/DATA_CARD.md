## Data Integrity Notes

1. **No real customer PII**: All client names, voice notes, and memory fields are fictional.
2. **Trend labels grounded in reality**: Trend themes (Quiet Luxury, 新中式×马术, etc.) are based on observable luxury market patterns, though scores are estimated.
3. **Product data is real**: Scraped from the official Celine website; used only for internal research/MVP.
4. **Traceability**: Every outreach output cites `evidence_used` (memory field + confidence) and `trend_id` references, enabling auditability.
